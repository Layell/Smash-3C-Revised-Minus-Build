Sheik Rework by Maye

These are just changes that I overheard a few PT want as well as some that I think would round out the character. Like most of the changes I do, this is intended to make her more fun to play against while also keeping her fun to play as.

Thanks,
Maye

Attributes:
	-Double Jump Multiplier increased x1 > x1.3
	-Total Number of Jumps decreased 3 > 2
	-Forward Air Landing Lag increased 10 > 12
	-Back Air Landing Lag increased 10 > 12
	-Up Air Landing Lag decreased 21 > 11
	-Down Air Landing Lag decreased 26 > 17
	
Forward Smash:
	-Second hit can no longer reverse
	
Up Smash:
	-Tipper hitbox size decreased 5.5 > 4.5
	-Tipper damage decreased 13% > 11%
	
Forward Air:
	-KBG increased 74 > 85
	
Back Air:
	-Strong hit angle changed 20 > 361
	-Strong	hit damage decreased 14% > 12%
	-Weak hit KBG decreased 100 > 80
	
Up Air:
	-Strong hit damage increased 13% > 14%
	-Strong hit BKB increased 30 > 35
	-Strong hit duration increased 3 > 6 (frames) 
	-Weak hit BKB increased 10 > 20
	
Back Throw:
	-Angle changed 112 > 125
	-Total throw damage increased 7% > 10%
	
Forward Throw:
	-Total throw damage increased 7% > 10%
	
Down Throw:
	-Angle changed 80 > 60
	-KBG increased 50 > 65
	-Replaced Attack cancel on frame 43 with an IASA

Neutral Special:
	-No longer explodes on the ground
	
Side Special:
	-No longer gives Sheik a Vertical or Horizontal boost anymore
	
Up Special:
	-Travel Frames increased 20 > 23
	-Can now grab ledge during travel
